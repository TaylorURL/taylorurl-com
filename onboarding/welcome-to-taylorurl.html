4 Your client portal

Every TaylorURL client gets an account in the portal at launch — and often much earlier. The portal is where the marketing site you're paying for proves it's working.

Inside the portal you'll see:

  • Visitor counts — how many real people are reaching your site.
  • Page views — which pages are pulling weight and which need attention.
  • Uptime percentage — independent verification that your site is online.
  • Status badges — quick visual signals when something needs a look.

Three roles exist in the portal: client (you and the people you invite), staff (the internal role Trenton uses when working on your account), and admin (also Trenton). New signups default to the client role. TaylorURL is a one-man shop, so staff and admin are the same person — me. Full walkthrough lives in the Your Client Portal guide.
